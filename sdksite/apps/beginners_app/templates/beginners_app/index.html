About the Allen Brain Atlas SDK

The 'Allen Brain Atlas SDK' is an open-data platform that allows you to explore our publicaly available data and tools. Here we have created multiple data resources, each of which will allow you to discover different insights into the brain works! As you get comfortable with the structure and organization of our data, feel free to go the extra mile and explore connections between the components of the different resources. Or even compare our data against your own!

What Are Our Data Resources?

Our data resources are a growing collection of online public resources integrating extensive gene expression and neuranatomical data. Currently we have made available three of these resources, each of which will provide you with a unqiue look into the brain:

  • Brain Observatory: explore sensory processing in the mouse visual cortex
  • Cell Types DB: observe the electrophysiological and morphological properties of individual neurons in the mouse primary visual cortex
  • Mouse Connectivity Atlas: Understand the complex architecture of neuronal connections in the mouse brain
