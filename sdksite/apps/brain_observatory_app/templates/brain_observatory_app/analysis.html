Analysis Overview

Our Brain Observatory project reveals how neurons encode different visual stimuli in both single cells and populations of cells found within mouse visual cortex.

But what can we do with this data? We can explore functional properties of neurons in the brain area by looking at spatial and temporal response characteristics of visual neurons. With this, we can then come one step closer to answering how neurons encode visual information.
